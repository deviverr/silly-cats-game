Silly Cats City — Quick Guide:
  • Controls: Move with WASD or arrow keys. Press Space to jump.
  • Camera: press C or click the Camera button to cycle views (3rd / 1st / Orbit). Use mouse wheel to zoom in 1st/3rd view.
  • Play modes: singleplayer or multiplayer rooms. Use the Start menu to join or create a room.
  • Objective: play silly missions (example: find cats), interact and enjoy the quirky world.
Note: Your player is represented by a simple cat model. NPC cats wander the map.
